How to issue your own

          

            
1. Generate keys and issuer profile JSON
              (badge-cli onboard-issuer).

            
2. Host /.well-known/openbadges-issuer.json on
              your domain.

            
3. Verify domain: POST /public/api/issuers/verify.
            

            
4. Issue and sign your first badge, then share verify link.
