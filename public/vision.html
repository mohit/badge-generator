Creating the Let's Encrypt Moment for Digital Credentials

We're at a tipping point for digital credentials. Just like Let's Encrypt democratized HTTPS, we're building the infrastructure to make verifiable credentials free, open, and ubiquitous.

🚀 Why Now?

Digital badges and credentials are rapidly gaining traction across industries:

of employers favor skills-based hiring
$15B

2.5x
faster hiring with verified micro-credentials
89%

  • Employers increasingly favor micro-credentials over traditional degrees for specific skill verification
  • Educational institutions and corporate training providers are eager to demonstrate granular competencies that traditional transcripts miss
  • Market projections estimate the digital credentialing industry to grow significantly over the next decade

Yet, current growth is happening largely within proprietary ecosystems, limiting universal interoperability, raising costs, and hindering true democratization.

🌟 Our Vision: The Let's Encrypt Model for Credentials

Just as Let's Encrypt created a simple, open, and cost-free standard for HTTPS, we envision a world where digital credentials are completely democratized.

❌ Current Problems

  • Fragmented, proprietary ecosystems
  • High costs preventing small issuer adoption
  • Limited interoperability across platforms
  • Weak security and verification standards
  • Vendor lock-in limiting credential portability

✅ Our Solution

  • Completely free and accessible to organizations of all sizes
  • Universal interoperability with seamless cross-platform integration
  • Cryptographically robust standards ensuring authenticity
  • Open source preventing vendor lock-in
  • Standards-compliant with W3C and Open Badges specifications

🔬 Proof-of-Concept Validation

We've successfully validated the technical feasibility of this vision using Open Badges v3:

✅ Technical Achievements

  • Issuer Setup: Instantly created fully compliant issuer ("Test Learning Academy")
  • Badge Class Creation: Easily defined badges with clear criteria and metadata
  • Credential Issuance: Seamlessly issued verifiable digital credentials
  • Cryptographic Verification: Implemented Ed25519 digital signatures
  • API-Driven Automation: Demonstrated bulk badge issuance capabilities

🛡️ Security & Standards

  • Fully compliant with Open Badges v3 and W3C Verifiable Credentials
  • Hosted badge components publicly using universally verifiable JSON-LD data
  • Implemented cryptographic signatures for tamper-proof verification
  • Created automated verification systems with multi-level security checks
